Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How does the Password Generator work?

The tool uses a secure algorithm to generate a password by randomly combining characters based on your selected criteria like length and character types.

Can I generate passwords with specific characters?

Yes, you can choose to include uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols according to your security needs.

Are these passwords stored or saved anywhere?

No, all generated passwords are created on your browser and are never saved, stored, or transmitted, ensuring your complete privacy.

Why should I use a password generator?

Using a generator helps avoid weak or reused passwords. It provides strong, unpredictable passwords that are difficult for hackers to guess or crack.
