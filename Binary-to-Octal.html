What is a binary to octal converter?

A binary to octal converter is a tool that transforms binary (base-2) numbers into octal (base-8) format quickly and accurately.

How does the conversion from binary to octal work?

Binary digits are grouped in sets of three from right to left. Each triplet is then converted to its octal equivalent, forming the final result.
