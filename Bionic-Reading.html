Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is Bionic Reading?

Bionic Reading is a technique that highlights the first few letters of each word to guide your eyes and improve focus and reading speed.

How does this tool work?

The tool scans your text, bolds the optimal number of letters based on a fixation setting, and displays the transformed text for easier reading.

Can I customize the bold text style or color?

Yes, you can customize the bold text color, regular text color, font family, and fixation level to match your preferences.
